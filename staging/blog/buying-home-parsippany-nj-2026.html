Market Overview

As a seasoned real estate agent in Morris County, I, Jorge Ramirez, can attest that the Parsippany market is thriving. The median home price in Parsippany is around $440,000, with a mix of modern and traditional homes. The current market trend shows a steady increase in property values, making it an excellent time to invest in a home here.

Neighborhoods

Parsippany is home to several distinct neighborhoods, each with its own charm. Some of the notable areas include Mount Tabor, Powder Mill, and Troy Hills. These neighborhoods offer a range of living options, from cozy single-family homes to spacious estates, ensuring there's something for every buyer's preference.

Schools

The Parsippany-Troy Hills School District is renowned for its commitment to education, providing students with a comprehensive learning experience. With a strong focus on academic achievement and extracurricular activities, families can feel confident about the quality of education their children will receive.

Commute to NYC

For those who need to commute to New York City, Parsippany offers a convenient location. The average commute time to NYC Penn Station is approximately 45 minutes by car and around 60 minutes by public transportation, making it an attractive option for professionals working in the city.
