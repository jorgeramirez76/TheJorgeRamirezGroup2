Neighborhoods in Short Hills

Short Hills has several distinct neighborhoods, each with its own character. Some of the most popular neighborhoods include:

  • Brick Township: Known for its beautiful parks and tranquil atmosphere.
  • Millburn Township: Famous for its excellent schools and family-friendly environment.
  • Union Township: A bustling neighborhood with a mix of old and new homes.

NJ County Schools — Short Hills School District

The Short Hills School District is highly rated, with several schools receiving blue ribbon awards. As a parent, you can rest assured that your child will receive a top-notch education in this district.

Commute from Short Hills to NYC

The commute from Short Hills to NYC is approximately 45 minutes via the NJ Transit train, making it an attractive option for those who work in the city but want to live in a more suburban area.
