Buying a Home in Verona, NJ 2026 — Complete Buyer's Guide

Market Overview

Welcome to Verona, a charming town in Essex County, New Jersey! As a local expert and top NJ agent, I'm excited to share my knowledge with you. The current median home price in Verona is around $420,000, with a mix of colonial-style homes, modern constructions, and vintage properties. The market is relatively stable, with a slight increase in prices over the past year.

Neighborhoods

Verona is comprised of several distinct neighborhoods, each with its own character. Some of the most notable areas include:

  • Forest Hill, known for its tree-lined streets and historic homes
  • Verona Park, a family-friendly area with a strong sense of community
  • Montrose, featuring a mix of modern and vintage properties

These neighborhoods offer a range of lifestyles and architectural styles, ensuring that buyers can find the perfect fit for their needs.

Schools

Verona is served by the Verona Township School District, which is dedicated to providing a high-quality education to its students. The district comprises several schools, including elementary, middle, and high schools, ensuring that children receive a comprehensive education from kindergarten through graduation.

Commute to NYC

One of the significant advantages of living in Verona is its commute time to New York City. The journey to Penn Station takes approximately 35-40 minutes by train, making it an attractive option for those working in the city. This convenience, combined with the tranquility of suburban living, makes Verona an excellent choice for commuters.
