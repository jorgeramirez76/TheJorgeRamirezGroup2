Highland Park NJ Market Snapshot — March 2026

The current market in Highand Park, NJ is characterized by a median home price of $430,000, with an average of 25-35 days on market. The inventory level is moderate, and the year-over-year price change is +5%.

  • Median Home Price: $430,000
  • Average Days on Market: 25-35 days
  • Inventory Level: moderate
  • Year-over-Year Price Change: +5%

Year-over-Year Price Trends

The year-over-year price change of +5% indicates a steady increase in home prices in Highand Park, NJ. This trend suggests that the market is balanced, with neither buyers nor sellers having a significant advantage.

This increase in home prices can be attributed to the town's diverse community, proximity to Rutgers University, and the New Brunswick dining scene. The demand for homes in Highand Park, NJ is expected to remain strong, driven by its attractive location and amenities.

Neighborhood Breakdown

The neighborhoods in Highand Park, NJ can be characterized by their unique price ranges and demand. The Raritan River waterfront area is known for its expensive homes, with prices ranging from $500,000 to over $1 million. The South 2nd Ave historic district is a popular area for families, with home prices ranging from $300,000 to $500,000.

The Donaldson Park neighborhood is a highly sought-after area, with prices ranging from $400,000 to $700,000. The Woodbridge Ave corridor is an up-and-coming area, with prices ranging from $250,000 to $400,000. Each neighborhood offers a distinct lifestyle and amenities, catering to different buyer preferences.

What's Driving Demand in Highand Park

The commute time to NYC, schools, and lifestyle are significant factors driving demand in Highand Park, NJ. The town's proximity to Rutgers University and the New Brunswick dining scene makes it an attractive location for buyers.

The commute time of 50-60 minutes via NJ Transit bus or ~20 minutes to New Brunswick station (NEC line) is a major draw for buyers who work in NYC. The Highland Park School District and notable schools like Highland Park High School also contribute to the town's appeal.
