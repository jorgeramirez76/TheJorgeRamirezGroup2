Market Overview

As a seasoned real estate agent in Morris County, NJ, I, Jorge Ramirez, can attest that the Mountain Lakes market is highly competitive, with a median price of around $525,000. The area offers a mix of modern and traditional homes, with many featuring beautiful lake views.

Neighborhoods

Mountain Lakes, NJ is comprised of several unique neighborhoods, each with its own character. Some of the most popular areas include The Hills, known for its upscale homes and scenic views; Mountain Lakes Lake, which offers stunning lakefront properties; and Brittany Road, a family-friendly neighborhood with tree-lined streets. Other notable areas are Oakwood, Alpine, and the historic district near the lake.

Schools

Families in Mountain Lakes, NJ are served by the Mountain Lakes School District, which includes schools such as Mountain Lakes Elementary, Briarcliff Elementary, and Mountain Lakes High School. These schools are known for their strong academic programs and community involvement.

Commute to NYC
