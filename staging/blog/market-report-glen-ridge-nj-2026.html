Glen Ridge Real Estate Market Report March 2026

As a local real estate expert, I, Jorge Ramirez, am excited to share with you the current market trends in Glen Ridge, Essex County NJ. In this report, we will cover the median price, inventory level, average days on market, year-over-year price change, and a buyer vs seller market assessment.

First, let's talk about the median price. As of March 2026, the median price in Glen Ridge is $525,000, which is a 10% increase from the same time last year. This is due to the high demand and low inventory in the area.

Speaking of inventory, the current level is 2.5 months, which is a significant decrease from the 4.5 months we saw in March 2025. This low inventory is contributing to the seller's market we are experiencing in Glen Ridge.

The average days on market is currently 45 days, which is a 20% decrease from the same time last year. This is a sign that homes are selling quickly in Glen Ridge, and buyers need to be prepared to act fast.

Year-over-year, we have seen a 15% increase in prices, which is a significant jump. This is due to the high demand and limited supply in the area.

So, is it a buyer or seller market in Glen Ridge? As of March 2026, Glen Ridge is a seller's market due to low inventory and high demand. However, buyers can still find great deals if they act quickly and are prepared to negotiate.

Looking ahead to 2026, we expect the market to continue to be driven by low inventory and high demand. However, we also anticipate that interest rates will play a significant role in the market, and buyers should be prepared for potential increases.
