Market Overview

As a seasoned real estate agent in Union County, I'm excited to share my expertise with you. The Rahway market is thriving, with a median home price of $425,000. With its rich history, vibrant downtown, and easy access to major highways, Rahway is a hidden gem that's worth exploring.

Neighborhoods

Rahway is comprised of several distinct neighborhoods, each with its own character. Some of the most popular areas include Winfield, Pershing, and North Rahway. These neighborhoods offer a range of housing options, from historic homes to modern condos, and provide easy access to local amenities like parks, restaurants, and shops.

Schools

Families will appreciate the highly-rated Rahway Public Schools district, which serves the community with a strong commitment to education. With a focus on academic excellence and extracurricular activities, Rahway's schools provide a solid foundation for students of all ages.

Commute to NYC

One of the major advantages of living in Rahway is its convenient commute to New York City. With the Rahway Train Station just a short walk away, you can be in Penn Station in under 30 minutes, making it an ideal choice for professionals who work in the city. The commute time is approximately 35-40 minutes, depending on traffic and the time of day.
