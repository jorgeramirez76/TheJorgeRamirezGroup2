Buying a Home in Springfield NJ 2026 — Complete Buyer's Guide

Market Overview

As a top real estate agent in Union County, I've seen the Springfield market thrive in recent years. The current median home price in Springfield is around $430,000, with a mix of colonial-style homes, modern constructions, and vintage properties. If you're looking to buy or sell a home, now is an excellent time to explore the market.

Neighborhoods

Springfield is divided into several charming neighborhoods, each with its unique character. Some of the most popular areas include Baltic Street, Jerome Street, and Mountainview Road. These neighborhoods offer a range of housing options, from family-friendly homes to trendy apartments. For instance, the area around Baltic Street is known for its tree-lined streets and historic homes, while Jerome Street is popular for its proximity to local parks and playgrounds.

Schools

Families in Springfield can enjoy the excellent schools within the Springfield Public School District. This district is renowned for its commitment to academic excellence and extracurricular activities, making it an attractive option for parents looking to relocate. Specifically, the district offers a range of programs, including gifted and talented classes, as well as sports teams and clubs.

Commute to NYC

One of the significant advantages of living in Springfield is its commute time to New York City. The journey from Springfield to NYC Penn Station takes approximately 45 minutes by train, making it an ideal location for professionals who work in the city but prefer a suburban lifestyle. This commute time is based on the average travel time during peak hours, and it's worth noting that traffic and train schedules can vary, so it's always a good idea to check for the most up-to-date information.
