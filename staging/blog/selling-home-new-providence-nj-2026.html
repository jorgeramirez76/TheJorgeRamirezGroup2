Neighborhoods in New Providence

New Providence boasts several desirable neighborhoods, each with its unique appeal. Downtown New Providence offers a walkable lifestyle with easy access to the train station. The Glenwood section and Hillcrest area are known for their family-friendly environments and proximity to top-rated schools.
