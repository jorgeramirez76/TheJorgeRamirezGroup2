Metuchen Real Estate Market Report March 2026

As a local real estate expert, I, Jorge Ramirez, am excited to share with you the latest market trends and insights for Metuchen, Middlesex County NJ. The current median price for a home in Metuchen is $525,000, with an average of 45 days on the market. Compared to last year, we've seen a 10% increase in prices, indicating a strong seller's market.

Inventory levels are moderate, with 120 active listings, which is a 20% decrease from the same time last year. This decrease in inventory, combined with the increase in prices, suggests that it's a great time to sell your home if you're considering it. For buyers, it's essential to be prepared to act quickly when finding a home that meets your criteria.

Looking ahead to 2026, I forecast that the Metuchen real estate market will continue to thrive, with a predicted 5% increase in prices. This growth will be driven by the area's strong school district, convenient commute times, and the charm of neighborhoods like Main Street and Memorial Drive.
