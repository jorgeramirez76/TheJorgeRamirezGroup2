Neighborhoods

Kenilworth is comprised of several unique neighborhoods, each with its own character. Some of the most notable areas include:

  • Netherwood Heights: Known for its beautiful tree-lined streets and historic homes.
  • The Reservation: A serene neighborhood surrounded by lush greenery and the Rahway River.
  • Kenilworth Village: The heart of the town, featuring a mix of old and new constructions, with a strong sense of community.

These neighborhoods, among others, make Kenilworth a diverse and appealing place to live.

Commute to NYC

One of the major advantages of living in Kenilworth is its convenient commute to New York City. The town is approximately 22 miles from NYC Penn Station, with a commute time of around 35-40 minutes by car, depending on traffic. Public transportation options are also available, including bus and train services.
