Linden, Union County NJ Real Estate Market Report for March 2026

As a seasoned real estate agent, I, Jorge Ramirez, have been tracking the market trends in Linden, Union County NJ, and I'm excited to share my insights with you. The current median price for a single-family home in Linden is around $430,000, with an average days-on-market of 45 days.

The inventory level is relatively low, with only 120 active listings, which is a 15% decrease from the same time last year. This decrease in inventory has led to a seller's market, where buyers are competing for limited properties.
