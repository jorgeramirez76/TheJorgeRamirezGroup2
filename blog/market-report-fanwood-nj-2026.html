The Fanwood, NJ real estate market is characterized by a median home price of $560,000, with an average of 18-25 days on market. The current inventory level is low, indicating a seller's market. Year-over-year, prices have increased by 7%. Here's a quick stats block to illustrate the current market conditions:

  • Median Home Price: $560,000
  • Average Days on Market: 18-25 days
  • Inventory Level: low
  • Year-over-Year Price Change: +7%

As we delve into the specifics of the Fanwood market, it's essential to understand the dynamics at play. The commute to NYC via the NJ Transit Raritan Valley Line to Penn Station takes approximately 55-65 minutes, making Fanwood an attractive option for those working in the city. The Scotch Plains-Fanwood Regional School District serves the area, with notable schools like Scotch Plains-Fanwood High School, which is top-rated in Union County.

Year-over-Year Price Trends

The 7% year-over-year price increase in Fanwood indicates a steady growth in the market. This increase suggests that the area is becoming more desirable, likely due to its small-town charm, walkable downtown, and tight community feel. As a result, buyers can expect to pay a premium for homes in this area, while sellers can anticipate benefiting from the increased demand.

Neighborhood Breakdown

Fanwood's neighborhoods each have their unique character and price points. Downtown Fanwood, with its walkability to the train station, tends to have higher price points, ranging from $500,000 to over $700,000 for renovated or newer homes. The Martine Ave area and LaGrande neighborhood offer a mix of older and newer homes, with prices ranging from $450,000 to $650,000. South Fanwood, being a bit further from the town center, sees prices from $380,000 to $580,000. Demand is high across all neighborhoods due to the area's desirability and limited inventory.

Buyer vs Seller Market Analysis

Given the current market type as a seller's market, buyers face a more competitive environment. They should be prepared to act quickly when finding a suitable property, as homes are selling within 18-25 days on average. Sellers, on the other hand, are in a favorable position, with the potential to secure higher sale prices due to the low inventory levels and high demand. However, sellers should also be mindful of the market's dynamics and price their homes competitively to attract buyers.

For the rest of 2026, buyers and sellers in Fanwood can expect the market to continue its trend as a seller's market.
