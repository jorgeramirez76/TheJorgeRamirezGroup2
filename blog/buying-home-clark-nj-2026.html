Neighborhoods

Clark boasts several beautiful neighborhoods, each with its own unique charm. Jericho Road, for instance, is known for its tree-lined streets and picturesque homes. Parkside, on the other hand, is a family-friendly area with a strong sense of community. And if you're looking for a more rural feel, Raritan offers a quieter lifestyle. Schools

Clark is served by the Arthur L. Johnson Elementary School, Edward Fabishak Elementary School, and the Arthur L. Johnson Middle School, all part of the Clark Public Schools district. For high school, students attend Arthur L. Johnson High School, which is also part of the Clark Public Schools district. How important is the quality of the school system to you in your search?
