Buying a Home in Clark NJ 2026 — Complete Buyer's Guide

Welcome to Clark, Union County NJ, a charming town with a strong sense of community! As a top NJ agent at Keller Williams, I'm excited to share my expertise with you. In this buyer's guide, we'll explore the market overview, neighborhoods, schools, commute to NYC, and what buyers should know when purchasing a home in Clark.

Market Overview

The current median price for a home in Clark is around $430,000, with a mix of colonial-style homes, modern constructions, and vintage properties. As of 2026, the market is seeing a moderate increase in demand, driven by its excellent school system and convenient location. If you're considering buying, now is an excellent time to do so.

Neighborhoods

Clark boasts several beautiful neighborhoods, including:

  • Jericho Road: Known for its tree-lined streets and picturesque homes.
  • Parkside: A family-friendly area with a strong sense of community.
  • Raritan: Offering a more rural feel, perfect for those seeking a quieter lifestyle.

Schools

Clark is served by the Arthur L. Johnson Elementary School, Edward Fabishak Elementary School, and the Arthur L. Johnson Middle School, all part of the Clark Public Schools district. For high school, students attend Arthur L. Johnson High School, which is also part of the Clark Public Schools district.

Commute to NYC

The commute from Clark to NYC is approximately 45 minutes via car or around an hour by public transportation, making it an attractive option for those working in the city but seeking a suburban lifestyle.
