One of the biggest draws to Randolph is the Randolph Township School District. Commute to NYC

For NYC commuters, Randolph's location is a key advantage. The typical commute is via Drive to NJ Transit or Midtown Direct Coach bus (~50–60 min to NYC). With hybrid and remote work arrangements common, many buyers today can absorb a slightly longer commute in exchange for Randolph's quality of life and home values.

Here's what I tell every buyer I work with in Randolph:
