Commute to NYC

For those who need to commute to NYC, Bloomfield offers a convenient option. The typical commute is via NJ Transit bus (Bloomfield Ave line) ~45–55 min to NYC. What Buyers Should Know in 2026

There are a few important considerations to keep in mind as you navigate the Bloomfield real estate market:

  1. Get pre-approved before you look — in a balanced market, sellers won't take an offer seriously without it.
  2. Know your neighborhood priorities — factors like commute distance, school zone, lot size, and walkability vary by block.
  3. Be ready to move fast — desirable homes in Bloomfield often receive offers within the first weekend. Having your documents ready is crucial.
  4. Budget for property taxes — New Jersey's property taxes are significant. Make sure to factor the full PITI payment into your budget.
  5. Don't skip the inspection — many Bloomfield homes are 50–80 years old; a thorough inspection can protect you.
