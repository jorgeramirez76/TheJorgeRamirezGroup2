Neighborhood Breakdown

Mountainside's neighborhoods each have their unique charm and price ranges. Here's a breakdown of what you can expect in each area:

  • Mountainside Country Club area: This enclave of luxury homes is characterized by its sprawling lots and high-end finishes. Prices in this neighborhood range from $900,000 to over $1.5 million.
  • Deer Path Road corridor: This picturesque area is known for its tree-lined streets and classic homes. Prices here range from $600,000 to $1.2 million.
  • Surprise Lake section: This tranquil neighborhood is beloved for its serene lake views and wooded lots. Homes in this area range from $500,000 to $1 million.
  • Springfield Ave area (border with Springfield): This charming neighborhood is marked by its convenient location and affordable prices, ranging from $400,000 to $800,000.

What's Driving Demand in Mountainside

So, what's behind the demand in Mountainside? Let's look at the commute, schools, and lifestyle that make this area so appealing:

  • Commute: While there's no direct train to NYC, the ~15-minute drive to Westfield NJ Transit station (Raritan Valley Line) and the option to drive to NYC via I-78 (40-55 minutes) make it a viable choice for commuters.
  • Schools: The Mountainside School District (K-8) and Governor Livingston High School (grades 9-12) are highly regarded, making this area a top choice for families.
  • Lifestyle: The Watchung Reservation border, large wooded lots, and very low density make Mountainside an ideal choice for those seeking privacy and prestige.
