Parsippany Real Estate Market Report March 2026

As a local real estate expert, I, Jorge Ramirez, have been tracking the Parsippany market closely. The median price for a home in Parsippany is currently $525,000, which is a 2.5% increase from the same time last year. The inventory level is moderate, with around 120 active listings, and the average days on market is 45 days.

The market is currently a balanced one, with neither buyers nor sellers having a significant advantage. However, with the average days on market decreasing by 10% year-over-year, it's an indication that the market is slightly shifting towards a seller's market. If you're a buyer, it's essential to act quickly when you find a home you like, as the market is competitive.

Looking ahead to 2026, I expect the Parsippany market to continue its moderate growth, with a forecasted 3.5% increase in median price. This growth will be driven by the area's strong economy, excellent schools, and convenient commute times. The average commute time from Parsippany to New York City is around 45 minutes, making it an attractive option for professionals working in the city.
