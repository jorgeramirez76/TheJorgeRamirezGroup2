What Buyers Are Looking For in Verona Right Now

Have you thought about what buyers are looking for in Verona? They often seek a combination of lifestyle, convenience, and community. The commute to NYC, which is approximately 50-60 minutes via NJ Transit bus or a 10-minute drive to Montclair Bay Street NJ Transit station for a direct line to NYC, is a significant draw. The excellent schools in the Verona Public Schools district, including Verona High School, are also a major attraction for families. The lifestyle in Verona, with its beautiful park, strong local business district, and tight-knit community, appeals to those seeking a charming suburban life with easy access to amenities.

Neighborhoods in Verona

Do you know about the unique neighborhoods in Verona? The Verona Park area is beloved for its serene lakefront and accessibility to outdoor activities. The Forest Ave corridor and Claremont Ave area are popular for their convenient location and local amenities. The Bloomfield Ave commercial strip offers a vibrant mix of shops and eateries, while the Upper Verona / Cedar Grove border area provides a tranquil, more secluded living experience. Understanding the strengths of each neighborhood can help in marketing your home to the right buyer.
