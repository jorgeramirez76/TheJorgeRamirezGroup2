Market Conditions

As of 2026, the Denville real estate market is experiencing a moderate pace, with the average home spending around 45-60 days on the market. The median price for a home in Denville is approximately $425,000, with a slight increase in prices compared to the previous year. Commute time to nearby cities like Parsippany and Morristown is around 20-30 minutes, making Denville an attractive option for those working in the surrounding areas.

Neighborhoods like Downtown Denville, Beacon Hill, and Indian Head Road are highly sought after for their charm, proximity to amenities, and community events. The local school district, Denville Township Schools, is also a significant draw for families.

Preparation Tips

Preparing your home for sale requires attention to detail and a strategic approach. Here are some tips specific to Denville:

  • Enhance curb appeal by maintaining your lawn and exterior
  • Stage your home to appeal to a wide range of buyers
  • Address any needed repairs to avoid price negotiations
  • Utilize high-quality photos and virtual tours to showcase your property
