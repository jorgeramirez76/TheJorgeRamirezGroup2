Have you ever thought about the unique charm of Mountainside, Union County, NJ? Home Preparation Tips Specific to Mountainside

Help me understand, have you considered how the natural beauty of Mountainside can enhance your home's appeal? Buyers are drawn to this area for its privacy, wooded lots, and low crime rate. When preparing your home for sale, focus on highlighting these features. Emphasizing the natural light and scenic views from your windows can be a major selling point. Additionally, highlighting the convenient commute options, such as the proximity to the Westfield NJ Transit station and the drive to NYC via I-78, can attract buyers looking for a balance of privacy and convenience.

What Buyers Are Looking For in Mountainside Right Now

How open would you be to learning more about what buyers truly want in Mountainside? They are often seeking a combination of privacy, natural beauty, and convenience. The commute to NYC, whether via the Raritan Valley Line from Westfield or driving, is a significant factor. The excellent schools, including those in the Mountainside School District and Governor Livingston High School, are also a major draw for families. The lifestyle in Mountainside, with its low crime rate and Watchung Reservation, appeals to buyers seeking a peaceful and prestigious living environment.

Neighborhoods in Mountainside

Mountainside boasts several unique neighborhoods, each with its own charm. The Mountainside Country Club area offers a serene and exclusive living environment, perfect for those seeking privacy and luxury. The Deer Path Road corridor provides easy access to natural areas and scenic views. The Surprise Lake section is known for its beautiful landscapes and peaceful atmosphere. Lastly, the Springfield Ave area, bordering Springfield, combines the benefits of a Mountainside address with the convenience of being near the town's amenities.

FAQ

Q: What is the current median home price in Mountainside?

A: The current median home price in Mountainside is $780,000.

Q: How long does it take to commute to NYC from Mountainside?

A: The commute to NYC from Mountainside can be done via the Raritan Valley Line from Westfield, taking approximately 70 minutes, or by driving via I-78, which takes around 40-55 minutes.

Q: What school district do children in Mountainside attend?

A: Children in Mountainside attend schools in the Mountainside School District for grades K-8 and then attend Governor Livingston High School in Berkeley Heights for grades 9-12.

Q: What is the property tax rate in Mountainside?

A: The property tax rate in Mountainside is approximately 1.9%, which is one of the lowest in Union County.
