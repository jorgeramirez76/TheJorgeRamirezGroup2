Neighborhood Breakdown

Metuchen's neighborhoods each have their unique charm and price points. Downtown Metuchen, with its walkable access to the train and a variety of shops and restaurants, commands higher prices, typically ranging from $600,000 to $800,000 for a 3-bedroom home. The Middlesex Ave corridor and Lincoln Ave area offer a mix of older and newer constructions, with prices between $450,000 and $700,000. Woodwild Park, known for its serene and family-friendly atmosphere, sees prices from $500,000 to $750,000. What's Driving Demand in Metuchen

The factors driving demand in Metuchen are multifaceted. The commute to NYC via the NJ Transit NEC line, which takes about 42-50 minutes from Metuchen Station to Penn Station, is a significant draw. The town's highly rated schools, including Metuchen High School, are another major attraction. The lifestyle offered by Metuchen, with its walkable downtown and low crime rate, also makes it a desirable place to live. These elements combined create strong demand for properties in the area.
