Neighborhoods

Linden is comprised of several distinct neighborhoods, each with its own unique character. Some of the most popular neighborhoods include:

  • Warrenville: Known for its tree-lined streets and historic homes, Warrenville is a charming neighborhood that offers a suburban feel.
  • Linden Heights: This neighborhood is characterized by its mix of older and newer homes, offering a range of options for buyers.
  • Georgetown: With its mix of apartments and single-family homes, Georgetown is a vibrant and diverse neighborhood.

Schools

Linden is served by the Linden Public Schools district, which includes schools such as Linden High School, Myles J. Rowe Elementary School, and Windsor H. Sutherland Elementary School. The school district is known for its strong programs and dedicated teachers, making it an attractive option for families.

Commute to NYC
