Denville Real Estate Market Report March 2026

Last updated  ·  April 2026

Out of curiosity, have you been keeping an eye on the real estate market in Denville, Morris County NJ? It's a place that has been steadily growing, and I've been tracking its trends closely. In this report, I'll dive into the current market dynamics, median prices, and more to give you a clearer picture.

The median price for a home in Denville is currently around $525,000, marking a 2.5% increase from the same period last year. The inventory level stands at about 120 active listings, a 15% decrease from the same time last year. On average, homes are spending around 45 days on the market, which is a 10% decrease compared to last year.

Year-over-year, we've seen a 5% increase in prices, indicating a stable and growing market. However, the current buyer vs. seller market assessment slightly favors buyers, with a buyer-to-seller ratio of 1.2:1. This is due to the moderate inventory levels and the relatively slow price growth.

Looking ahead to 2026, I forecast continued growth in the Denville real estate market, with a predicted median price increase of around 3-4%. This growth will be driven by the ongoing development of the town's infrastructure, including new commercial and residential projects, as well as the town's excellent school district and convenient commute time to major cities.
