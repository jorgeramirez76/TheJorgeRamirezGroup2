Scotch Plains NJ Real Estate Market Report 2026

Scotch Plains, NJ is a charming township in Union County, offering a diverse range of neighborhoods that cater to different lifestyles and preferences. As a homebuyer, it's essential to explore the various areas to find the perfect fit for your needs. Here's a spotlight on 5 distinct neighborhoods in Scotch Plains, highlighting their unique vibe, price range, and suitability for different demographics. **1. Downtown Scotch Plains** Downtown Scotch Plains is the hub of the township, filled with shops, restaurants, and community events. This area has a lively, small-town feel, with a mix of old and new architecture. The price range for homes in Downtown Scotch Plains varies from $400,000 to $700,000. This neighborhood is ideal for families and young professionals who want to be close to amenities and public transportation. The area is also suitable for downsizers who want to transition into a more compact, walkable lifestyle. **2. The Manor** The Manor is an upscale neighborhood in Scotch Plains, characterized by large, luxurious homes on sprawling properties. This area exudes an elegant, suburban vibe, with tree-lined streets and ample green spaces. Home prices in The Manor range from $800,000 to $1,200,000. The Manor is best suited for families with a medium to high budget, seeking a tranquil, upscale environment with excellent schools and community amenities. **3. Parkway Area** The Parkway Area is a charming, laid-back neighborhood in Scotch Plains, named after the nearby Parkway Avenue. This area has a relaxed, suburban atmosphere, with a mix of older and newer homes. Prices for homes in the Parkway Area range from $350,000 to $600,000. This neighborhood is perfect for first-time homebuyers, singles, and families on a budget, who want a peaceful, residential environment with easy access to local amenities. **4. Netherwood Area** The Netherwood Area is a highly sought-after neighborhood in Scotch Plains, known for its beautiful, wooded properties and scenic views. This area has a natural, serene ambiance, with an emphasis on outdoor recreation and community events. Home prices in the Netherwood Area range from $500,000 to $900,000. The Netherwood Area is ideal for families with school-age children, who want a safe, picturesque environment with excellent schools and outdoor recreational opportunities. **5. Willow Avenue Area** The Willow Avenue Area is a quiet, residential neighborhood in Scotch Plains, characterized by a mix of older and newer homes. This area has a cozy, small-town feel, with a strong sense of community and local pride. Prices for homes in the Willow Avenue Area range from $300,000 to $550,000. This neighborhood is perfect for downsizers, singles, and budget-conscious families, who want a peaceful, low-maintenance lifestyle with easy access to local amenities. In conclusion, Scotch Plains, NJ offers a diverse range of neighborhoods that cater to different lifestyles and preferences. Whether you're a family, single, or downsizer, there's a perfect area for you in this charming township.
