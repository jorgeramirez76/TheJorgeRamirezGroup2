The current market trend in Edison, NJ is a seller's market, with low inventory and high demand. As a result, homes are selling quickly, and buyers need to be prepared to act fast. The median home price has increased by 10% in the past year, making it essential for buyers to work with a knowledgeable agent like myself to navigate the market.

Neighborhoods

Edison, NJ has several unique neighborhoods, each with its own character. Some of the most popular neighborhoods include:

  • Park Terrace: Known for its tree-lined streets and charming homes, Park Terrace is a favorite among families and first-time buyers.
  • Oak Tree: This neighborhood is popular for its convenient location, with easy access to shopping and dining options.
  • Green Brook: With its picturesque views and tranquil atmosphere, Green Brook is a sought-after neighborhood for those seeking a peaceful living environment.

Schools

The Edison Township School District serves the area, with several highly-rated schools, including Edison High School, which has a reputation for academic excellence. As a parent and a real estate agent, I can attest to the importance of a strong school district in determining the quality of life for families.

Commute to NYC

One of the significant advantages of living in Edison, NJ is its convenient commute to New York City. The train ride from the Edison train station to Penn Station takes approximately 45-60 minutes, making it an ideal option for those working in the city. This commute time is a major factor in the decision-making process for many buyers, and I'm happy to provide guidance on the best ways to navigate the commute.
