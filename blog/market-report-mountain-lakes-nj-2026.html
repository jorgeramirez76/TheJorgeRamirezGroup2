Mountain Lakes NJ Market Snapshot — March 2026

The current state of the real estate market in Mountain Lakes, NJ, as of March 2026, is characterized by a median home price of $760,000, with homes spending an average of 25-35 days on the market. The inventory level is very low, indicating a seller's market. The year-over-year price change stands at +6%, reflecting a steady increase in property values.

Stats Block:

Year-over-Year Price Trends

The +6% year-over-year price change signifies a robust growth in the local real estate market. This increase is indicative of the high demand and limited supply that characterize Mountain Lakes' real estate landscape. As a result, homeowners can expect a significant return on their investment, while buyers should be prepared for a competitive market.

Neighborhood Breakdown

Mountain Lakes is comprised of distinct neighborhoods, each with its unique charm and price range. The Lakeside Drive area, known for its stunning lakefront homes, tends to have higher price points. The Boulevard area and Midvale section offer a mix of modern and traditional homes, with prices adjusting accordingly. The Pines neighborhood, with its picturesque setting, also commands a premium. Understanding these neighborhood dynamics is crucial for both buyers and sellers to navigate the market effectively.

Buyer vs Seller Market Analysis

Given the current market conditions, Mountain Lakes presents a seller's market. This means that sellers are in a advantageous position, with their properties likely to attract multiple offers and potentially selling above the asking price. Buyers, on the other hand, face a more challenging environment, where they must be prepared to act quickly and potentially negotiate on price. Despite these dynamics, both parties can find success by being informed and adaptable.

What's Driving Demand in Mountain Lakes

The demand in Mountain Lakes is driven by several key factors, including its commute accessibility via the NJ Transit Morris & Essex Line to Penn Station, which offers a commute time of 70-85 minutes. The top-ranked schools within the Mountain Lakes School District, such as Mountain Lakes High School, are a significant draw for families. The lifestyle offered by the community, with its idyllic lake settings and low crime rate, also attracts buyers seeking a tranquil and safe environment. The combination of these factors contributes to the area's desirability and the resultant market conditions.

2026 Forecast

For the remainder of 2026, buyers and sellers in Mountain Lakes can expect the market to continue its trend of steady growth. The low inventory levels and high demand are anticipated to persist, potentially leading to further price increases. However, the pace of these changes may stabilize as the year progresses, offering opportunities for both sides of the transaction to achieve their goals. Staying informed about market developments and working with a knowledgeable local real estate expert will be key to navigating these conditions successfully.
