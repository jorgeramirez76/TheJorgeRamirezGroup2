Mountain Lakes NJ Market Snapshot — March 2026

Have you been following the real estate market in Mountain Lakes, NJ, and wondering how it's shaping up? As of March 2026, the market is quite active, with a median home price of $760,000 and homes spending an average of 25-35 days on the market. The inventory level is very low, indicating a seller's market, and the year-over-year price change stands at +6%, reflecting steady growth.

Year-over-Year Price Trends

Out of curiosity, have you noticed the +6% year-over-year price change? This growth highlights the high demand and limited supply in Mountain Lakes. It suggests that homeowners could see a significant return on their investment, while buyers might need to be more competitive in their offers.

Neighborhood Breakdown

What's been the biggest challenge with navigating the different neighborhoods in Mountain Lakes? The Lakeside Drive area, known for its lakefront homes, tends to have higher price points. The Boulevard area and Midvale section offer a mix of modern and traditional homes, with prices that vary. The Pines neighborhood, with its picturesque setting, also commands a premium. Understanding these dynamics can help both buyers and sellers make informed decisions.

Buyer vs Seller Market Analysis

Given the current market conditions, Mountain Lakes is clearly a seller's market. This means that sellers are likely to attract multiple offers and potentially sell above the asking price. Buyers, however, may need to act quickly and be prepared to negotiate. Both sides can find success by staying informed and adaptable.

What's Driving Demand in Mountain Lakes

Help me understand, what factors are you considering when thinking about Mountain Lakes? The commute accessibility via the NJ Transit Morris & Essex Line to Penn Station, offering a 70-85 minute trip to Penn Station, is a significant draw. The top-ranked schools within the Mountain Lakes School District, such as Mountain Lakes High School, are another major factor. The community's idyllic lake settings and low crime rate also make it an appealing choice for those seeking a tranquil and safe environment.

2026 Forecast

How open would you be to exploring the forecast for Mountain Lakes in 2026? The market is expected to continue its trend of steady growth, with low inventory levels and high demand likely to persist.
