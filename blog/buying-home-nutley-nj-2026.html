Commute to NYC

For NYC commuters, Nutley's location is a key advantage. The typical commute is via NJ Transit bus routes (~45–55 min to NYC via Port Authority). With hybrid and remote work arrangements common, many buyers today can absorb a slightly longer commute in exchange for Nutley's quality of life and home values.

What Buyers Should Know in 2026

Here's what I tell every buyer I work with in Nutley:

  1. Get pre-approved before you look — in a balanced market, sellers won't take an offer seriously without it.
  2. Know your neighborhood priorities — commute distance, school zone, lot size, and walkability all vary by block.
  3. Be ready to move fast — desirable homes in Nutley often receive offers within the first weekend. Have your documents ready.
  4. Budget for property taxes — New Jersey's property taxes are significant. Factor the full PITI payment into your budget.
  5. Don't skip the inspection — many Nutley homes are 50–80 years old; a thorough inspection protects you.
