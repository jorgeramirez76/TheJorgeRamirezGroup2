Neighborhoods in Fanwood

Help me understand, are you looking for a specific type of neighborhood in Fanwood? The town is comprised of several unique areas, each with its own character. The Downtown Fanwood area is a popular choice for its walkability to the train station, while the Martine Ave area offers a mix of old and new homes. The LaGrande neighborhood is known for its beautiful tree-lined streets, and the South Fanwood area provides a more suburban feel. All of these neighborhoods offer a range of housing options, from modern apartments to historic single-family homes.

Commute from Fanwood to NYC

What's been the biggest factor in your decision to consider homes in Fanwood?
