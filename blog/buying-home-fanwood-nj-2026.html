Market Overview

The current market in Fanwood, NJ is highly competitive, with a mix of modern and traditional homes available. As of 2026, the median home price is $525,000, with homes ranging from cozy colonials to spacious modern designs. With its strong school district and convenient commute to NYC, Fanwood is an excellent choice for families and professionals alike.

Neighborhoods

Fanwood, NJ is home to several unique neighborhoods, each with its own character. Some of the most popular neighborhoods include:

  • Nomahegan Park: Known for its beautiful park and walking trails, Nomahegan Park is a favorite among nature lovers.
  • Scotch Plains: This neighborhood offers a mix of modern and traditional homes, with easy access to local amenities.
  • Oakwood Heights: With its tree-lined streets and classic homes, Oakwood Heights is a charming choice for families.

Schools

The best schools in Fanwood, NJ are part of the Scotch Plains-Fanwood School District, which includes schools like Park Middle School and Scotch Plains-Fanwood High School. These schools are known for their strong curriculum and excellent teachers, making them a top choice for families with children.

Commute to NYC

The commute time from Fanwood, NJ to NYC Penn Station is approximately 45-60 minutes by train, making it an attractive option for professionals who work in the city. With its convenient location and easy access to major highways, Fanwood is an excellent choice for those who need to balance work and family life.
