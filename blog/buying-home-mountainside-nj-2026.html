Market Overview

The Mountainside, NJ real estate market is thriving, with a mix of older and newer homes, from colonial to modern styles. As a buyer, you'll have a range of options to choose from, including homes with large yards, private decks, and beautiful views. The median price of a home in Mountainside, NJ is around $640,000, making it an attractive option for those looking to buy in the area.

Neighborhoods

Mountainside, NJ is home to several beautiful neighborhoods, each with its own unique character. Some of the most popular neighborhoods include:

  • Deerfield: Known for its tree-lined streets and classic colonial homes, Deerfield is a favorite among families.
  • Hillcrest: This neighborhood boasts stunning views of the surrounding countryside and a range of modern home styles.
  • Oakcrest: With its picturesque streets and charming homes, Oakcrest is a sought-after neighborhood for those looking for a peaceful place to live.

Schools

Mountainside, NJ is served by the Mountainside School District, which is known for its excellent schools and strong focus on education. As a parent, you can rest assured that your child will receive a top-notch education in Mountainside, NJ.

Commute to NYC

One of the major advantages of living in Mountainside, NJ is its easy commute to NYC. The train ride from Mountainside to NYC Penn Station takes approximately 45-60 minutes, making it an ideal option for those who work in the city but want to live in a more suburban area.
