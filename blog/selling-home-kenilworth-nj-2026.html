Neighborhoods in Kenilworth

Kenilworth is divided into several distinct neighborhoods, each with its own appeal. The Boulevard area is a main residential corridor, known for its beautiful homes and tree-lined streets. South Kenilworth, near the Union border, offers a mix of older and newer homes, with a strong sense of community. North Kenilworth, near the Cranford border, is popular for its easy access to the NJ Transit train station and the vibrant downtown areas of neighboring towns. The Kenilworth industrial district, located in the southwest, is an area of commercial and industrial development, offering opportunities for businesses and entrepreneurs. When selling your home, it's essential to understand the unique appeal of your neighborhood and how it fits into the broader lifestyle and amenities that buyers are looking for.
