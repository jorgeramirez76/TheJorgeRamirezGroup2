Pricing your home correctly is crucial. In Basking Ridge's market, homes that are priced accurately attract the most interest in the first 7–10 days, often securing the best and highest offers. Preparing Your Home for Sale

At Basking Ridge's price points, buyers have high expectations. Here are some key steps to make your home stand out:

  • Curb appeal: First impressions start online, with 85% of buyers searching on their phones first. Professional photos after decluttering and fresh landscaping are essential.
  • Declutter and depersonalize: Help buyers visualize themselves in the home by reducing furniture and personal items.
  • Address deferred maintenance: Fix obvious issues before listing. Buyers often use problems as negotiating leverage.
  • Consider staging: Professionally staged homes tend to sell faster and for more in this market.
