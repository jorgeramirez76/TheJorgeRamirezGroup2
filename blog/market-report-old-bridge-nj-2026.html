Old Bridge Real Estate Market Report - March 2026

As a local real estate expert, I, Jorge Ramirez, am pleased to provide you with the most up-to-date information on the Old Bridge, Middlesex County NJ real estate market. In this report, I will cover the current median price, inventory level, average days on market, year-over-year price change, and a buyer vs seller market assessment.

The current median price in Old Bridge is $425,000, which is a 2.5% increase from the same time last year. The inventory level is moderate, with approximately 120 active listings. Homes are selling quickly, with an average of 45 days on market. This is a significant decrease from the 60 days we saw last year.

Year-over-year, we have seen a 5% increase in prices, indicating a steady growth in the market. However, the market is still slightly tilted in favor of buyers, with a buyer vs seller ratio of 1.2:1. This means that for every 1.2 buyers, there is 1 seller, giving buyers a slight advantage in negotiations.

Looking ahead to 2026, I forecast a continued growth in the Old Bridge real estate market. With low interest rates and a strong economy, buyers are likely to remain active, driving up demand and prices. However, I also expect to see an increase in inventory, which could help to balance out the market and give sellers more negotiating power.
