**Counties Covered:** Union, Hudson, Middlesex, Morris, Essex

**Research Date:** February 2026

## 1. PROPERTY RECORDS

### 1.1 NJ Tax Records (Statewide)
- **URL:** https://www.njtaxrecords.net/
- **Access:** Web search, free public access
- **Data Available:** 
  - Property tax assessments
  - Ownership history
  - Recent sales data
  - Property characteristics (size, structure, year built)
  - Tax payment history
  - Assessed values
- **Update Frequency:** Annually (tax assessment cycle), with updates for transactions
- **Coverage:** All 21 NJ counties including Union, Hudson, Middlesex, Morris, Essex

### 1.2 NJ Property Transparency Portal (State)
- **URL:** https://nj.gov/transparency/property/
- **Access:** Interactive web map
- **Data Available:**
  - Property parcel information
  - Location and owner information
  - Block and lot information
  - Annual assessed value (land and building)
  - Prior year's taxes
  - Property classification
- **Update Frequency:** Annual (January filings)
- **Coverage:** Statewide

### 1.3 County-Specific Tax Record Search

#### Union County
- **URL:** https://ucnj.org/taxation/ (general taxation info)
- **Access:** Via municipal tax assessors or County Board of Taxation
- **Data Available:** Tax assessments, appeals
- **Coverage:** All 21 municipalities in Union County

#### Hudson County
- **URL:** https://www.hcnj.us/finance/tax-assessments/ (informational)
- **Note:** Each municipality maintains its own tax records
- **Coverage:** 12 municipalities including Jersey City, Hoboken, Bayonne

#### Middlesex County
- **URL:** Contact individual municipalities
- **Tax Board:** https://www.middlesexcountynj.gov (general)
- **Coverage:** 25 municipalities

#### Morris County
- **URL:** https://mcweb1.co.morris.nj.us/MCTaxBoard/SearchTaxRecords.aspx
- **Access:** Web search by property
- **Data Available:** Tax records, assessment information
- **Coverage:** 39 municipalities

#### Essex County
- **URL:** Individual municipality assessor offices
- **County Tax Board:** Part of Essex County government
- **Coverage:** 22 municipalities including Newark, Montclair, Bloomfield

- **Data Available:**

## 3. TAX DELINQUENCIES / TAX SALES

### 3.1 Municipal Tax Sales
- **Access:** Individual municipality tax collector offices
- **Data Available:** 
  - Tax sale certificates (liens on property)
  - Delinquent municipal charges
  - Outstanding water/sewer bills
- **How it Works:** NJ municipalities hold annual tax sales where tax sale certificates (liens) are sold to investors
- **Coverage:** All municipalities (tax sales are municipal-level, not county)
- **Note:** Tax sale lists published by each municipality typically in late spring/early summer

### 3.2 NJ Division of Local Government Services
- **URL:** https://www.nj.gov/dca/divisions/dlgs/
- **Access:** Informational documents
- **Data Available:** Tax sale process documentation, regulations

## 5. LEGAL RECORDS

### 5.1 Federal Bankruptcy Court (PACER)
- **URL:** https://pacer.uscourts.gov/
- **Access:** Free account registration; fees for document access (waived under $30/quarter)
- **Data Available:**
  - Bankruptcy case filings
  - Docket entries
  - Case summaries
  - Party information
- **Cost:** $0.10/page, max $3.00/document; FREE if under $30/quarter
- **Update Frequency:** Real-time
- **Coverage:** Federal District of New Jersey (includes all 5 counties)
- **Alternative:** Voice Case Information System (free via phone 866-222-8029)

### 5.2 PACER Case Locator
- **URL:** https://pcl.uscourts.gov/
- **Access:** Free with PACER account
- **Data Available:** Nationwide federal case search
- **Update:** Daily at midnight

### 5.3 NJ State Court Records (Civil)
- **Access:** NJ Court system does NOT have centralized free online access to civil case records
- **Options:**
  1. Visit courthouse in person (free to view)
  2. Use third-party services (fees apply)
  3. Contact NJ Courts for specific case inquiries
- **Coverage:** Superior Court cases by county
- **Note:** Divorce filings are generally NOT publicly accessible online in NJ; require in-person visit or court order

### 5.4 Probate Records (Surrogate's Court)
- **Access:** County Surrogate's Court offices
- **Data Available:** 
  - Estate records
  - Wills (if filed for probate)
  - Estate inventories
  - Executor appointments
- **How to Access:** 
  1. Visit County Surrogate's office in person
  2. Some counties may have online search (limited)
  3. Mail requests
- **Coverage:** County-level
- **Cost:** Usually free to view, fees for copies

#### Surrogate's Court Locations:
| County | Surrogate | Location |
|--------|-----------|----------|
| Union | Judge | Elizabeth |
| Hudson | Judge | Jersey City |
| Middlesex | Judge | New Brunswick |
| Morris | Judge | Morristown |
| Essex | Judge | Newark |

- **URL:** https://data.census.gov
- **Access:** Free web access, API available
- **Data Available:**
  - American Community Survey (ACS) data
  - Decennial Census data
  - Age distributions
  - Housing characteristics
  - Owner/renter data
  - Income and poverty data

- **Access:** Free web search
- **Data Available:** Aggregated property statistics by county

## DATA ACCESS REALITY CHECK

### What's ACTUALLY Available for FREE:
1. ✅ Property tax assessments (statewide)
2. ✅ Foreclosure/sheriff sale listings (all counties)
3. ✅ Building permit statistics (state-level aggregated)
4. ✅ Bankruptcy filings (federal PACER - minimal cost)
5. ✅ Demographics/Census data (comprehensive)
6. ✅ Deed/mortgage records (searchable, fees for copies)

### What's DIFFICULT to Get FREE:
1. ⚠️ Divorce filings (not online, court visit required)
2. ⚠️ Active/expired/withdrawn MLS listings (private databases)
3. ⚠️ FSBO data (fragmented, no comprehensive source)
4. ⚠️ Detailed tax delinquency lists (municipal, varies)
5. ⚠️ Individual building permits (municipal level, fragmented)

### What's NOT Available FREE:
1. ❌ Real-time listing status (expired/withdrawn)
2. ❌ Historical MLS data
3. ❌ Comprehensive FSBO tracking
4. ❌ Real-time divorce/probate updates
