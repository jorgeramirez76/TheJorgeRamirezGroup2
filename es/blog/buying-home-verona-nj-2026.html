Neighborhoods in Verona

Verona comprises several distinct neighborhoods, each with its unique character. The Verona Park area, with its lakefront properties, offers a tranquil living environment. The Forest Ave corridor and Claremont Ave area provide easy access to local amenities and services. The Bloomfield Ave commercial strip is the hub de la town's shopping and dining experiences. Lastly, the Upper Verona / Cedar Grove border area presents a mix of residential and natural landscapes.
