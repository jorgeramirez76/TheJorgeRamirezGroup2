Condado de Essex Bienes Raíces Market Overview

Condado de Essex is one of Nueva Jersey's most dynamic bienes raíces markets, anchored by the Midtown Direct train line that gives commuters a direct ride to Midtown Manhattan. This single piece of infrastructure drives billions of dollars in home values across the county.

The county's western half — Millburn, Short Hills, Livingston, North Caldwell, and Verona — is defined by large-lot suburban living with nationally ranked school districts. Short Hills routinely appears on lists of America's wealthiest ZIP codes. Livingston draws families with its strong schools and relative value compared to Millburn.

The eastern corridor — Montclair, Glen Ridge, Maplewood, and South Orange — attracts buyers who want walkability, culture, and character. Montclair's downtown rivals any small city for dining and nightlife. Maplewood's village district has become a destination for young families leaving Brooklyn and Manhattan. South Orange offers Seton Hall University, a charming downtown, and Midtown Direct access.

For buyers seeking entry points into Condado de Essex, Bloomfield and Nutley offer solid housing stock at a fraction of Montclair and Glen Ridge prices. Both towns have seen significant appreciation as buyers priced out of more expensive neighbors look for value with good transit access. West Orange's diverse neighborhoods span a wide price range, with luxury sections rivaling Millburn and more affordable areas offering strong starter-home opportunities.
