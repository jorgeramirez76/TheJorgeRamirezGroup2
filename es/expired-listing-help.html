The 3 Reasons Casas Don't Sell

After years of working with sellers whose previous listings expired, Jorge has found that failed listings almost always come down to one — or a combination — of these three factors. None of them are your fault.

1. Overpriced From Day One

This is the number one reason homes sit. Your previous agent may have told you what you wanted to hear about your home's value to win the listing — then spent months chasing the market down with price reductions. Buyers notice. A home that has been reduced multiple times signals desperation, and serious buyers either lowball or skip it entirely. The right price on day one is not just a number — it is a strategy that creates urgency, competition, and leverage. Jorge's data-driven pricing is built on comparable sales, pending data, and investor-level analysis — not wishful thinking.

2. Under-Marketed and Under-Exposed

Listado your home on the MLS and waiting is not a marketing strategy. It is a hope strategy. If your previous agent relied on a yard sign, a few photos taken on a phone, and an open house or two, your home never reached the buyers who would have paid top dollar for it. The majority of buyers start their search online — and if your listing did not reach them through targeted digital advertising, it was invisible to them. Jorge's AI-powered marketing system proactively finds buyers instead of waiting for them to stumble across your listing.

3. Poor Agent Communication and Follow-Up

How many times did your last agent call you with an update? How quickly did they respond to buyer inquiries? How many showing requests went unanswered or were scheduled days late? Poor follow-up kills deals before they start. Buyers move fast — if their agent cannot reach your agent within hours, they move on a la next property. Jorge works seven days a week, answers his phone directly, and uses automated systems to ensure no lead, no showing request, and no buyer inquiry goes unanswered.
