How It Works

Unlike traditional email marketing, AI Email doesn't send the same template to everyone. It learns from each lead's behavior and customizes their entire sequence.

1. Lead arrives — They visit your site or inquire about a property

2. AI reads intent — What did they search? What property? First-time buyer or investor?

3. Sequence created — Your AI builds a unique sequence for THIS lead

4. Email 1 drops — Personalized subject, personalized content

5. AI monitors & adapts — They didn't open? Email 2 is different. They clicked a link? Email 3 shifts focus.
