FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about buying and selling homes in New Jersey

How many communities do you serve in New Jersey?

I serve 103 communities across 5 counties in Northern and Central New Jersey, including Essex County (11 towns), Hudson County (12 towns), Morris County (37 towns), Middlesex County (22 towns), and Union County (21 towns).

What is your experience as a real estate agent?

I've been a full-time realtor with Keller Williams Premier Properties since 2017, bringing 8+ years of dedicated experience helping clients buy and sell homes across Northern New Jersey. Additionally, I've personally fixed and flipped over 60 houses throughout New Jersey, giving me hands-on experience with every aspect of the buying, renovation, and selling process.

Do you specialize in NYC commuter properties?

Yes! I specialize in helping clients find homes with excellent NYC commuter access via NJ Transit, including properties near Midtown Direct train lines in towns like Millburn, Summit, Maplewood, and Montclair.

What areas of New Jersey do you cover?

I primarily serve Northern and Central New Jersey, including: Essex County, Hudson County, Morris County, Middlesex County, and Union County. My office is located in Summit, NJ.

How can I get a free home valuation?

You can call or text me at 908-230-7844 for an immediate consultation, or fill out the contact form below. I provide comprehensive market analysis with comparative data to help you understand your home's true market value.

Do you work with first-time home buyers?

Absolutely! I work with buyers at all experience levels, from first-time buyers to luxury home purchasers and real estate investors. I provide guidance through every step of the home buying process. I also have an established network of trusted professionals including loan officers, attorneys, home inspectors, licensed plumbers, and licensed electricians to support you through every step.
