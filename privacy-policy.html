Last updated: April 5, 2026

Who We Are

The Jorge Ramirez Group is a real estate team at Keller Williams Premier Properties, located at 488 Springfield Avenue, Summit, NJ 07901. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website, thejorgeramirezgroup.com.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you interact with our website:

  • Contact information: Name, email address, phone number, and message content when you submit our contact form.
  • Property interest: Details about the type of real estate service you are interested in (buying, selling, home valuation, etc.).
  • Usage data: Pages visited, time on site, browser type, device type, and referring URL through Google Analytics.
  • Cookies: We use cookies for analytics purposes to understand how visitors use our site.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your inquiries about buying or selling real estate in New Jersey.
  • To provide home valuations and market analyses you request.
  • To improve our website and the services we offer.
  • To send follow-up communications related to your real estate inquiry (you can opt out at any time).

How We Protect Your Information

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information. Our website uses SSL encryption (HTTPS). We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Contact form submissions are sent directly to our licensed real estate agent via secure email.

Third-Party Services

Our website may use the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics: To analyze website traffic and usage patterns.
  • FormSubmit: To process contact form submissions.
  • Google Maps: To display our office location.
  • Google Fonts: To display web fonts.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal data.
  • Opt out of any marketing communications.
  • Request that we stop processing your personal data.
