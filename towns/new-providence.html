New Providence NJ Real Estate Market Overview 2026

New Providence is a small, family-oriented community that consistently ranks among the best places to live in New Jersey. Located approximately 22 miles west of Manhattan, New Providence offers excellent schools, safe neighborhoods, and convenient train access at prices more accessible than neighboring Summit or Chatham.

Current Market Statistics

  • Median Home Price: $725,000 – $875,000
  • Average Price per Sq Ft: $360 – $440
  • Average Days on Market: 25 – 45 days
  • Homes Selling Over Asking: 40-50%
  • Year-Over-Year Price Change: +2.0% to +3.5%
  • Inventory Level: 2.2 months (seller's market)

The New Providence real estate market is strong and consistent, supported by excellent schools and convenient NYC access. Well-priced homes often receive multiple offers from families seeking top-rated education.

Home Styles & Housing Stock in New Providence

New Providence offers diverse architectural styles:

  • Colonial Homes: The most prevalent style, featuring 3-4 bedrooms and ranging from 2,000-3,000 square feet.
  • Split-Level Homes: Popular in neighborhoods developed in the 1960s-1970s.
  • Ranch Homes: Found in older sections, popular with empty nesters.
  • Cape Cod Homes: Charming properties, many with additions.
  • New Construction: Limited tear-down and rebuild activity.

Typical Lot Sizes: New Providence lots typically range from 0.2-0.4 acres.
