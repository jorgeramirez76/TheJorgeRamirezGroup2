Chatham NJ Real Estate Market Overview 2026

Chatham is one of Morris County's most prestigious communities, offering exceptional schools, beautiful homes, and convenient NYC access. The area includes both Chatham Borough and Chatham Township, each with distinct character.

Current Market Statistics

  • Median Home Price: $900K – $1.3M
  • Average Price per Sq Ft: $425 – $550
  • Average Days on Market: 25 – 40 days
  • Inventory Level: 2.2 months (seller's market)
  • School Rating: A+ (Chatham School District)

Chatham Borough vs. Chatham Township

Chatham Borough: Historic downtown, walkable to train station, charming colonial homes, typically higher prices ($1M+)

Chatham Township: More land, newer construction, larger lots, excellent schools, slightly more affordable ($900K+)

NYC Commute from Chatham

Chatham offers convenient train service to NYC via NJ Transit's Morris & Essex Line:

  • To Penn Station: 45-55 minutes
  • Monthly Pass: ~$380
  • Peak Trains: Direct service during rush hour
  • Driving to NYC: 28 miles (45-60 minutes depending on traffic)

Both Chatham Borough and Township offer easy access to the Chatham train station, making this an ideal location for NYC professionals seeking suburban living.

Chatham Schools: Among the Best in NJ

The Chatham School District is consistently ranked among New Jersey's top districts:

  • District Rating: A+ (Niche.com)
  • Chatham High School: Ranked #15 in NJ, 98% graduation rate
  • Student-Teacher Ratio: 12:1
  • Test Scores: Significantly above state averages

Many families move to Chatham specifically for the schools, which directly impacts property values and demand.

Property Taxes in Chatham

Property taxes in Chatham vary between Borough and Township:
