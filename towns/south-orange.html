South Orange Real Estate FAQs

What's the difference between South Orange and Maplewood?

They share the same school district but have distinct characters. South Orange has the village center and SOPAC, while Maplewood has its own downtown. Both offer similar housing styles and values.

How long is the commute from South Orange to NYC?

The train takes 35-45 minutes to NYC Penn Station on the Midtown Direct line.
