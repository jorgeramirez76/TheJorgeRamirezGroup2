Millburn / Short Hills NJ Real Estate Market Overview 2026

Millburn Township, encompassing both Millburn and Short Hills, is consistently ranked as the #1 school district in New Jersey and one of the most affluent communities in the United States. Located approximately 20 miles west of Manhattan, this prestigious area attracts CEOs, finance professionals, and families seeking the absolute best in education and lifestyle.

Current Market Statistics

  • Median Home Price (Millburn): $1,100,000 – $1,400,000
  • Median Home Price (Short Hills): $1,300,000 – $1,800,000+
  • Average Price per Sq Ft: $480 – $650+
  • Average Days on Market: 25 – 45 days
  • Homes Selling Over Asking: 50-65%
  • Year-Over-Year Price Change: +2.5% to +4.5%
  • Inventory Level: 1.8 months (strong seller's market)

The Millburn/Short Hills real estate market is highly competitive and prestigious. Well-priced homes often receive multiple offers, sometimes with bidding wars. The combination of the #1 school district, convenient NYC commute, and luxury amenities creates sustained demand that supports strong property values.

Millburn vs. Short Hills

Millburn: The section near downtown and the train station features walkable access to shops and restaurants. Homes here include colonials, Tudors, and some newer construction. Prices typically range from $1M-$1.5M.

Short Hills: The more prestigious section featuring estate properties, larger lots, and some of New Jersey's most expensive homes. Short Hills offers privacy, luxury, and the coveted 07078 zip code. Prices typically range from $1.3M-$5M+.

Home Styles & Housing Stock in Millburn/Short Hills

Millburn and Short Hills offer exceptional architectural diversity and quality:

  • Colonial Homes: Classic center-hall colonials, many expanded and updated with luxury finishes. These range from 3,000-5,000+ square feet.
  • Tudor Homes: Distinctive properties with architectural character, particularly in older sections.
  • Modern & Contemporary: New construction and extensive renovations featuring open floor plans, high ceilings, and smart home technology.
  • Estate Properties: In Short Hills, magnificent homes on 1-3+ acre lots with pools, guest houses, and resort-like amenities ($2M-$5M+).
  • New Construction: Significant tear-down and rebuild activity, with new homes featuring the latest luxury finishes ($1.5M-$3M+).

Typical Lot Sizes: Millburn lots typically range from 0.25-0.4 acres, while Short Hills offers more generous lots of 0.5-2+ acres.
