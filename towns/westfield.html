Westfield Real Estate FAQs

What makes Westfield different from Summit?

Both have excellent schools and downtowns, but Summit has the direct Midtown Direct train (faster to NYC). Westfield offers slightly lower prices and an equally charming downtown experience.

How long is the commute from Westfield to NYC?

The train from Westfield to NYC takes 50-60 minutes with a transfer at Newark. Some residents drive to Summit (10 minutes) to catch the Midtown Direct train.

Are Westfield schools good?

Yes, Westfield Public Schools are consistently ranked among the top in Union County with A+ ratings across all schools.
