Scotch Plains NJ Real Estate Market Overview 2026

Scotch Plains is one of Union County's most diverse and welcoming communities, offering a wide range of housing options at prices more accessible than neighboring Westfield or Cranford. Located approximately 25 miles southwest of Manhattan, Scotch Plains attracts families seeking space, value, and excellent schools.

Current Market Statistics

  • Median Home Price: $650,000 – $800,000
  • Average Price per Sq Ft: $320 – $400
  • Average Days on Market: 30 – 50 days
  • Homes Selling Over Asking: 35-45%
  • Year-Over-Year Price Change: +2.0% to +3.5%
  • Inventory Level: 2.6 months (seller's market)

The Scotch Plains real estate market is steady and accessible, offering more value than neighboring towns. The diversity of housing stock means there's something for everyone, from first-time buyers to those seeking estate properties.

Home Styles & Housing Stock in Scotch Plains

Scotch Plains offers exceptional housing diversity:

  • Colonial Homes: The most common style, ranging from classic designs to expanded versions with 3,000+ square feet.
  • Historic Homes: Beautiful properties in the Fanwood-Scotch Plains Historic District, many dating to the 1700s-1800s.
  • Ranch Homes: Popular in neighborhoods developed in the 1950s-60s, offering single-level living.
  • Split-Level Homes: Common in 1960s-70s neighborhoods.
  • Cape Cod Homes: Charming properties, many with additions.
  • Estate Properties: Larger homes on 1+ acre lots, particularly in the western sections.
  • New Construction: In-fill development and tear-downs throughout the township.
  • Condominiums: Options for downsizers and first-time buyers.

Typical Lot Sizes: Range from 0.2-0.5 acres, with some estate properties on 1-3+ acres.
