Home Styles & Housing Stock in Montclair

Montclair is famous for its architectural diversity and beautiful historic homes:

  • Victorian Homes: Montclair's signature style – beautiful Queen Anne, Colonial Revival, and Shingle-style homes from the late 1800s and early 1900s.
  • Tudor Homes: Distinctive half-timbered properties with character and charm.
  • Colonial Homes: Classic center-hall designs from various eras.
  • Craftsman Bungalows: Arts and Crafts-style homes with built-ins and character.
  • Mid-Century Modern: Distinctive homes from the 1950s-60s with architectural significance.
  • Condominiums: Options in downtown areas and converted historic buildings.

Neighborhoods in Montclair

Upper Montclair

Character: Prestigious area with village center

Price Range: $1,000,000 – $1,800,000+

Best For: Buyers seeking prestige and walkability

Montclair's most exclusive section features beautiful homes near the Upper Montclair village with shops, restaurants, and the train station. Homes here include magnificent estates and historic properties.

Montclair Center / Downtown

Character: Urban living with arts and culture

Price Range: $600,000 – $1,200,000

Best For: Buyers seeking walkability and culture

Near the Wellmont Theater, art galleries, and restaurants, this area offers townhomes, condos, and historic homes with walkable access to everything.

Watchung Plaza Area

Character: Family-friendly near train station

Price Range: $700,000 – $1,100,000

Best For: Commuters and families

Near the Watchung Plaza train station, this neighborhood offers convenience and community with beautiful homes and easy NYC access.

South End / Forest Street

Character: More affordable with good value

Price Range: $550,000 – $850,000

Best For: First-time buyers and value seekers

The South End offers more accessible price points while still providing Montclair's amenities and access to Bloomfield Avenue shops.

Schools: Magnet Excellence

The Montclair Public School District uses a magnet school model:

Elementary Magnet Schools (K-5)

  • Bradford School: Environmental science magnet
  • Charles H. Bullock School: STEM and sustainability magnet
  • Edgemont Montessori School: Montessori education
  • Hillside School: Gifted and talented magnet
  • Nishuane School: Arts and global studies magnet
  • Watchung School: Science and technology magnet

Middle School (6-8)

  • Glenfield Middle School: A rating; diverse programs and strong academics.
  • Mount Hebron Middle School: A rating; STEM and arts focus.

High School (9-12)

  • Montclair High School: A+ rating; one of NJ's most diverse and acclaimed high schools. Notable for exceptional performing arts, strong academics, and championship athletics.
