Warren Township NJ Real Estate Market Overview 2026

Warren Township is Somerset County's destination for space and value. If you want a large home on a generous lot without the price tag of Essex or Morris County, Warren Township delivers. Located at the intersection of I-78 and I-287, Warren offers convenient access to everywhere.

  • Median Home Price: $750,000 – $950,000
  • Large Homes (3,500+ sq ft): $900,000 – $1,400,000
  • Average Price per Sq Ft: $280 – $360
  • Average Days on Market: 35 – 55 days
  • Homes Selling Over Asking: 30-40%

The Warren Township real estate market is steady and value-oriented. You get significantly more house for your money compared to towns closer to NYC, making Warren popular with families who prioritize space.
