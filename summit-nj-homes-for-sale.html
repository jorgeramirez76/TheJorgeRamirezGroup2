Summit NJ Neighborhoods

Summit's neighborhoods each have their own character and price point. Understanding the differences helps buyers find the right fit:

  • Downtown / Train Station Area: Maximum walkability, ideal for commuters. Condos, townhomes, and smaller colonials. Entry point for Summit buyers.
  • Brayton Hill: Known for beautiful historic homes and tree-lined streets. A mix of Victorians, Colonials, and Craftsmans with strong community pride.
  • Northside: Larger lots, newer construction, and spacious family homes. Premium pricing but exceptional space and privacy.
  • Prospect Street Corridor: Walkable to both downtown and schools. Popular with families and professionals who want easy access to Summit's amenities.
