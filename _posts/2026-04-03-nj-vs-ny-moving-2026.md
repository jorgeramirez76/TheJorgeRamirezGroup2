# NJ vs NY: Why More New Yorkers Are Choosing New Jersey in 2026

As a seasoned NJ real estate agent with Keller Williams Premier Properties, I've seen firsthand the shift in preferences among homebuyers from New York to our neighboring state. In this article, we'll explore the reasons behind this trend and what it means for buyers, sellers, and the entire NJ real estate market.

**The Rise of New Jersey**

According to data from the National Association of Realtors, New Jersey has seen a significant increase in home sales over the past few years, with some towns experiencing growth rates of up to 10% annually. This trend is not limited to specific regions; it's a statewide phenomenon that's attracting attention from buyers across the board.

**Why Choose New Jersey?**

So, what's driving this shift? Here are just a few reasons why more and more people are choosing New Jersey over New York:

1. **Affordability**: The cost of living in NJ is significantly lower than in NY, making it an attractive option for first-time buyers, families, and retirees.
2. **Quality of Life**: From the beaches to the mountains, New Jersey offers a diverse range of lifestyles and environments that cater to different interests and preferences.
3. **Access to NYC**: With several major highways and public transportation options connecting NJ to NYC, commuters can enjoy the best of both worlds without breaking the bank.
4. **Tax-Friendly**: NJ has a more competitive tax environment than NY, with lower property taxes and fewer restrictions on homeownership.

**Popular NJ Towns for Homebuyers**

Some of the most popular towns in NJ among homebuyers include:

* **Montclair**: Known for its vibrant arts scene, excellent schools, and stunning views of the Watchung Mountains.
* **Red Bank**: A charming coastal town with a rich history, beautiful parks, and easy access to NYC.
* **Middletown**: An affluent suburb with top-ranked schools, pristine beaches, and plenty of outdoor recreational opportunities.
* **Morristown**: A historic town with a thriving downtown area, excellent schools, and convenient access to NYC.

**Actionable Advice for Homebuyers**

If you're considering making the move from NY to NJ, here are some actionable tips to keep in mind:

1. **Research, research, research**: Look into different towns and neighborhoods to find the best fit for your lifestyle and budget.
2. **Work with a local expert**: A knowledgeable real estate agent can help you navigate the market, find the perfect property, and make informed decisions.
3. **Be prepared to act fast**: The NJ real estate market is highly competitive, so be ready to make an offer quickly when you find the right home.

**Conclusion**

The trend of New Yorkers choosing New Jersey over NY for their next home is clear: affordability, quality of life, access to NYC, and tax-friendliness are just a few reasons why more people are making the move.
