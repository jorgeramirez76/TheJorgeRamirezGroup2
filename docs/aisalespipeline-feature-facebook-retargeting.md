# Facebook Retargeting - Stop Wasting Ad Spend on Cold Audiences

## Only Chase Leads Who Already Raised Their Hand

Facebook Retargeting is the feature that turns wasted ad budget into qualified lead re-engagement. Instead of showing ads to cold audiences, you only retarget people who've already engaged with you — and the AI decides which ad message they see based on their behavior.

## What is Facebook Retargeting?

Facebook Retargeting automatically creates custom audiences of leads who've engaged with you (visited your site, called, texted, opened emails) and serves them targeted ads on Facebook. The system decides which message each person sees based on their engagement history and intent.

**Traditional Facebook ads:** $20-40 per lead. High spend, uncertain ROI.

**AI Retargeting:** $2-5 per lead. Warm audience, high intent. 300%+ ROI.

## How It Works

### Step 1: Lead Engagement
- Lead calls → Number tracked
- Lead texts → Phone captured
- Lead visits website → Pixel tracking
- Lead opens email → Engagement logged
- Lead responds to SMS → Interest confirmed

### Step 2: AI Builds Audience
- All engaged leads automatically added to retargeting list
- Segmented by engagement level (hot, warm, cold)
- Categorized by interest (buyer, seller, investor)

### Step 3: AI Selects Message
The AI decides which ad each person sees:
- **Hot lead (opened 3+ emails, visited site 5+ times):** "Ready to buy? Let's talk this week"
- **Warm lead (opened 1 email, visited once):** "Questions about homes in Millburn? Let me help"
- **Cold lead (one interaction):** "Still interested in that property? I have updates"

### Step 4: Ads Run
- Ads appear on Facebook/Instagram feed
- Only shown to people who've already engaged with you
- High intent = high conversion

### Step 5: Click & Convert
- Lead clicks ad
- Lands on personalized landing page (matched to their interest)
- Books appointment or schedules a call
- You follow up immediately
