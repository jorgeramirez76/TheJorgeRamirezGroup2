## üèÜ TOP TIER PLATFORMS (Start Here)

### 1. **Quora** (Domain Authority: 93)
**Why it's powerful:**
- Indexed heavily by Google (ranks for MILLIONS of keywords)
- AI platforms scrape Quora aggressively (ChatGPT cites it frequently)
- High trust signals (verified expert badges)
- Long-form answers rank well

**What to post:**
- Answer questions like:
  - "What should I know before buying a home in Summit, NJ?"
  - "How much do NJ real estate agents charge?"
  - "Is it worth hiring a realtor to sell my house?"
  - "What are closing costs in New Jersey?"
  - "Summit NJ vs Millburn NJ: which is better?"

**Format:**
- 300-500 word answers
- Lead with your expertise: "I'm a NJ Realtor, here's what I've seen..."
- Include data/examples from your experience
- Link to your blog posts (allowed in Quora)
- Add images/graphs (higher engagement)

**Time investment:** 30 min per answer, 2-3 answers/week  
**SEO impact:** High (backlinks + brand mentions)  
**AI SEO impact:** Very High (LLMs love Quora)

**Profile link:** quora.com/profile/[YourName]

## üéØ CONTENT DISTRIBUTION STRATEGY

**When you write a blog post, distribute it everywhere:**

1. Publish on your site (original)
2. Wait 24 hours (let Google index it first)
3. Republish on Medium (with canonical link)
4. Share on LinkedIn as article
5. Create Twitter thread summarizing key points
6. Answer related Quora question, link to blog
7. Post to relevant Facebook groups
8. Create Pinterest pins with blog images
9. Post on Google Business Profile
10. Send as Substack newsletter (if you have one)

**Result:** 1 blog post = 10 pieces of distributed content = 10X the SEO impact

## üí° AI SEO BEST PRACTICES

**To maximize AI platform visibility:**

1. **Use structured data** (you already have Schema.org on site)
2. **Answer questions directly** (AI loves Q&A format)
3. **Include data/numbers** ("Median price: $825K" not "expensive")
4. **Cite sources** (AI trusts sourced content)
5. **Use clear headings** (H2, H3 structure)
6. **Write naturally** (AI detects overly promotional content)
7. **Be the expert** ("In my 8 years selling NJ homes, I've seen...")
8. **Update regularly** (fresh content = higher AI relevance)
