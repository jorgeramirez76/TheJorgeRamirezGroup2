# Buyer Workflows - Intelligent Buyer Lead Qualification and Nurture

## Automate the Entire Buyer Journey From Lead to Closed Deal

Buyer Workflows are done-for-you automation sequences that take raw buyer leads and move them through your pipeline: qualification → nurture → ready for showing → appointment → close.

The AI handles the heavy lifting while you focus on showing homes and closing deals.

## What Are Buyer Workflows?

Buyer Workflows automatically:
- **Qualify** new buyer leads (timeline, budget, motivation)
- **Segment** them (first-time buyer, investor, upgrade, downsize)
- **Nurture** through SMS + email based on their segment
- **Alert you** when they're ready to see homes
- **Keep them warm** if they're not ready yet

## The Buyer Workflow System

### Lead Captured
Buyer inquires about a property or fills "buyer consultation" form.
- AI captures: Name, phone, email, property interest, timeline, budget (if provided)
- AI qualification begins immediately

### Automated Qualification (24 hours)
**Day 0-1:** Qualification SMS
- "Hi [name], thanks for your interest in the [property]. Quick question — what's your timeline to buy? (A) 30 days, (B) 2-3 months, (C) 6+ months?"
- Follow-up: "And what's your budget range?"
- Follow-up: "Are you a first-time buyer, or have you owned before?"

AI captures responses and updates lead score and segment.

### Segment-Specific Nurture
Based on answers, lead enters customized path:

**First-Time Buyer (High urgency):**
- Email: Educational content (down payment, closing costs, inspections)
- Email: Financing guide + pre-approval information
- SMS: "How open would you be to seeing homes this weekend?"
- Voice call: "Let me walk you through exactly what to expect"

**Investor/Cash Buyer:**
- Email: Investment opportunity analysis
- Email: Market analysis + ROI calculations
- SMS: "I have 3 investment properties that match your criteria"
- Voice call: "Let me show you the numbers"

**Upgrade/Downsize:**
- Email: Market analysis in their price range
- Email: Emotional/lifestyle benefits of their target area
- SMS: "Found 4 homes that check your boxes"
- Voice call: "Your equity has grown. Here's what's possible"

### Ready to Show
When buyer shows high engagement (opened 3+ emails, replied positively, mentioned timeline), AI alerts you:
**"Hot buyer ready to see homes - Jennifer Lee - Budget $400K - Timeline 30 days"**

You immediately reach out and schedule showings.

### Post-Showing Nurture
After they see homes:
- SMS: "Thoughts on the homes you saw?"
- Email: Personalized neighborhood guides for homes they visited
- Voice call: Answer questions, handle objections
- AI prepares offer support documents

### Follow-Up Through Close
- Offer preparation guidance
- Inspection coordination
- Appraisal monitoring
- Closing coordination
- All tracked in your CRM

## Workflow Customizations

- Triggers (what prompts the next step)
