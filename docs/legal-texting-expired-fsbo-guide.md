## üö® THE SHORT ANSWER

**Can you have AI text expired listings/FSBOs?**

**Legally**: Only if you have **prior express written consent** from each recipient.

**Without consent**: NO. You risk $500-$1,500 fines PER TEXT under TCPA (Telephone Consumer Protection Act).

**The loophole most agents try to use doesn't work** (see below).

## üìú TCPA BASICS (What You Must Know)

### What TCPA Prohibits:

**You CANNOT send automated texts (including AI-generated) to cell phones without:**
1. **Prior express written consent** (signed permission)
2. That specifically authorizes text messages
3. From you or your company

### What Counts as "Automated"?

- ‚úÖ AI sending texts (like GHL automation) = AUTOMATED
- ‚úÖ Bulk SMS tools = AUTOMATED
- ‚úÖ Text blasts = AUTOMATED
- ‚ùå You manually typing each text = NOT automated (allowed without consent)

### Penalties:

- **$500 per violation** (per text sent)
- **$1,500 per willful violation** (if you knew the rules)
- **Class action lawsuits** (FSBOs/expireds can sue collectively)
- **NAR Code of Ethics violations** (potential license issues)

## ‚ùå THE "ESTABLISHED BUSINESS RELATIONSHIP" MYTH

**What agents think:**
"They listed with a broker, so I have an established business relationship. I can text them."

**Why it's wrong:**

1. **EBR doesn't apply to automated texts** to cell phones (TCPA is strict)
2. **Their relationship was with THEIR agent**, not you
3. **Expired ‚â† consent** (they didn't give you permission)
4. **FSBO ‚â† consent** (public listing doesn't equal permission to text)

**The ONLY EBR exception** that works:
- You already have a relationship with that specific person (past client, referral who gave you their number, etc.)

## ‚úÖ HOW TO DO THIS LEGALLY

### Option 1: Get Written Consent First (Best Practice)

**Process:**
1. Send a **physical mailer** to expired/FSBO
2. Include a **consent form**: "Text YES to [your number] to get market updates"
3. When they text YES, **that's written consent**
4. NOW you can use AI/automation to text them

**Consent language (include in mailer):**
```
Text "YES" to (908) 230-7844 to receive text messages about your home's market value and selling strategies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out anytime.

By texting YES, you agree to receive automated text messages from The Jorge Ramirez Group.
```

**Why this works:**
- They initiated contact (texted YOU first)
- You have written proof of consent
- Fully TCPA compliant

---

### Option 2: Manual Texts Only (No AI, No Automation)

**Process:**
1. Pull expired/FSBO list from Espresso Agent
2. **Manually type** each text (no copy/paste, no automation)
3. Personalize every message
4. Send from your personal phone (not GHL)

**Example manual text:**
```
Hey [Name], saw your home at [Address] was listed with [Agent] last year. Still looking to sell? I've sold 3 homes on your street in the last 6 months. - Jorge
```

**Why this works:**
- Not automated (you're typing each one)
- TCPA doesn't apply to individual manual texts
- Still requires compliance with DNC list (see below)

**Downside:**
- Time-consuming (10-15 texts/hour max)
- Doesn't scale
- Can't use AI/automation

---

### Option 3: Call First, Get Verbal Consent, Then Text

**Process:**
1. **Call** the expired/FSBO (calls have different TCPA rules)
2. During call: "Can I text you information about [topic]?"
3. If they say YES, **note it in your CRM**
4. NOW you can text them (consent obtained)

**Script for call:**
```
"Hi [Name], this is Jorge Ramirez with Keller Williams. I see your home at [Address] was on the market recently. I've sold several homes in your neighborhood and wanted to reach out. Would it be okay if I texted you some recent sales data for your area?"
```

**If they say YES:**
- Follow up with text: "Thanks for the chat! Here's the info I mentioned: [link]"
- Add to GHL with tag: "Consent: Verbal (Date)"
- You can now automate follow-ups

**Why this works:**
- You got consent (even verbal counts for follow-up)
- TCPA allows calls to businesses + some residential (see below)
- Builds rapport first

## üìû NATIONAL DO NOT CALL (DNC) REGISTRY

**Separate rule from TCPA** (but also important):

### Can you call expired/FSBOs?

**YES, with conditions:**

1. **Check DNC list first**: scrubdnc.com or donotcall.gov
2. **If they're on DNC**: You can ONLY call if:
   - You have an established business relationship, OR
   - They gave you written permission

**Expired listing exception:**
- If they listed in last 18 months with ANY agent, you CAN call (even if on DNC)
- Considered "business inquiry" not "unsolicited"

**FSBO exception:**
- Advertising their home = "business inquiry"
- You CAN call (even if on DNC)

**After you call:**
- If they say "don't call again" ‚Üí add to YOUR DNC list (must honor)
- If they're interested ‚Üí get consent to text

## ‚úÖ COMPLIANCE CHECKLIST

**Before you send ANY automated texts:**

- [ ] Recipient gave **written consent** (text opt-in, form signature, etc.)
- [ ] Consent was **specific to text messages** (not just email)
- [ ] Consent was to **your company** (not another agent)
- [ ] Your GHL account has **proper opt-out handling** (auto-stop on "STOP")
- [ ] You're using **10DLC registered number** (GHL should handle this)
- [ ] Your texts include **your name + company** (not anonymous)
- [ ] Your texts have **opt-out instructions** ("Reply STOP to unsubscribe")
- [ ] You **keep records of consent** (for 4 years minimum)

**If you can't check ALL boxes ‚Üí Don't send automated texts.**

## üõ°Ô∏è HOW TO PROTECT YOURSELF

**If you DO automate texts (with consent):**

1. **Keep detailed records:**
   - Who opted in
   - How they opted in (text, call, form)
   - Date/time of consent
   - Proof (screenshot, recording, form submission)

2. **Include in every automated text:**
   - Your name + company
   - Opt-out instructions ("Reply STOP anytime")

3. **Honor opt-outs immediately:**
   - STOP, UNSUBSCRIBE, CANCEL, QUIT ‚Üí all trigger auto-unsubscribe
   - No texts after that (TCPA requirement)

4. **Use a registered 10DLC number:**
   - GHL should handle this (A2P 10DLC registration)
   - Verify with GHL support
   - Unregistered = carrier blocks you

5. **Don't text after 9pm local time:**
   - TCPA prohibits calls/texts outside 8am-9pm
   - GHL should have "quiet hours" setting
