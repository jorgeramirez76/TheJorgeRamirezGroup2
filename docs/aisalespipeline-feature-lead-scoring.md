# Lead Scoring - Intelligent Prioritization of Your Best Opportunities

## Never Waste Time on a Cold Lead Again

Lead Scoring is the system that separates hot leads from lukewarm ones — automatically. Your AI reads every signal (response speed, engagement level, questions asked, property interest) and assigns each lead a priority score. You focus on the 20% of leads that will close 80% of deals.

## What is Lead Scoring?

Lead Scoring is an intelligent prioritization engine that analyzes lead behavior in real-time and assigns a score (1-100) based on purchase intent. Leads with scores 80+ get your immediate attention. 40-60? They enter a nurture sequence. <40? AI handles them automatically until intent increases.

**What the AI reads:**
- Response speed (texted back within 5 minutes? Hot)
- Questions asked (specific property questions vs. "just browsing"?)
- Engagement level (opened 3 emails? Clicked a property link?)
- Market signals (searching price range? Neighborhood? School district?)
- Timing (first inquiry or 10th follow-up? Fresh interest or declining?)
- Property type (buyer looking vs. seller ready to list?)

## How It Works

### Real-Time Scoring

**Lead arrives:**
- Fills form: "Looking to buy 3-bed home in Millburn"
- AI scores: 65 (interested, but no timeline yet)

**Lead engages:**
- They reply: "Timely would be within 30 days"
- They click: "Show me homes under $500K"
- They respond: "Yes, interested in a call"
- **AI re-scores: 85** (hot lead - ready to move)

**You get alerted:**
- You see lead at top of priority list with "Hot" flag
- You call them immediately while they're thinking about it
- Result: 65% conversion to appointment

### Scoring Factors

| Signal | Points | Example |
|--------|--------|---------|
| **Response speed** | +5-15 | Replied within 5 min = +15 |
| **Timeline clarity** | +20-30 | "30 days" vs. "someday" |
| **Budget defined** | +10-20 | Stated budget vs. vague |
| **Property specificity** | +10 | Specific beds/bath/area |
| **Engagement pattern** | +5-20 | Opened 3+ emails = +20 |
| **Market readiness** | +15-25 | Pre-approved / searching actively |
