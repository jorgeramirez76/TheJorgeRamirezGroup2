# Seller Workflows - Automated FSBO, Expired, and Downsizer Outreach

## Purpose-Built Sequences for Every Seller Situation

Seller Workflows are pre-built, AI-powered automation sequences specifically designed for the three seller opportunities that generate 60-70% of agent commission: FSBO (For Sale By Owner), Expired Listings, and Downsizers.

Unlike generic CRM workflows, these sequences are optimized for each situation with timing, messaging, and follow-up patterns that actually work.

## What Are Seller Workflows?

Seller Workflows are done-for-you, customizable automation sequences that:
- **Identify** FSBO/Expired/Downsizer leads automatically
- **Qualify** them through intelligent SMS + email sequences
- **Move** them toward consultation
- **Convert** them to listing appointments

Each workflow is based on real agent data about what messaging and timing works best for each seller type.

## The 3 Seller Workflows

### 1. FSBO (For Sale By Owner) Workflow

**The Problem:** FSBOs are advertising their homes, but they're overwhelmed, uncertain about pricing, and nervous about missing something.

**Your Angle:** "I help FSBOs get top dollar AND eliminate the stress. Let me show you what I can do in 30 minutes."

**Workflow Sequence:**
- **Day 0:** Email with subject "Saw your listing on [address]" + market data
- **Day 1:** SMS: "Hi [name], quick question — how many showings have you had so far?"
- **Day 3:** If no response, SMS: "[Property] is in a hot market. I sold 3 similar homes last month for [price]. Worth a conversation?"
- **Day 5:** Email with case study: "Here's how I helped another FSBO sell for $50K over asking"
- **Day 7:** Voice call: "I have 2 serious buyers interested. Can we talk about positioning?"
- **Day 10:** Final email: "Last chance to see what we could do together"

**Customizations:**
- Adjust timing based on your market
- Change price points to match your area
- Add your own case studies/testimonials
- Modify "last contact" based on your follow-up preference

**Expected Results:**
- Response rate: 30-40%
- Consultation booking: 15-25% of responses
- Listing rate: 40-50% of consultations

### 2. Expired Listing Workflow

**The Problem:** Their listing just expired. They're frustrated, embarrassed, and uncertain what to do next.

**Your Angle:** "I know exactly why it didn't sell and how to fix it. Let me help you get it sold this time."

**Workflow Sequence:**
- **Day 0 (same day listing expires):** Email with subject "Your listing just expired — here's the opportunity"
- **Day 0:** SMS with market data: "Your property is in a hot area. Let me show you what changed in 90 days."
- **Day 1:** Voice call with personalized approach (using AI Voice)
- **Day 2:** Email with price analysis: "Here's what similar homes sold for last quarter"
- **Day 4:** SMS: "I have 3 active buyers for [property type]. Schedule 15 min?"
- **Day 6:** Email with "re-marketing strategy": New photos, repositioning, price adjustments
- **Day 8:** Final SMS: "Still interested in selling? I have one more buyer interested."

**Customizations:**
- Add your comps/price data automatically
- Change buyer count based on your actual leads
- Adjust messaging tone (aggressive vs. educational)
- Add video walkthrough (if you have one)

**Expected Results:**
- Response rate: 35-50%
- Consultation booking: 20-30% of responses
- Listing rate: 50-60% of consultations

### 3. Downsizer Workflow

**The Problem:** Downsizers are emotional about leaving, uncertain if it's the right time, and need help understanding the financial upside.

**Your Angle:** "You've earned the freedom to downsize. Let me show you how much equity you have and what's possible."

**Workflow Sequence:**
- **Day 0:** Email: "Ready for the next chapter? Let's talk about your options"
- **Day 2:** SMS with emotional appeal: "Downsizing isn't giving up — it's upgrading your lifestyle"
- **Day 3:** Email with financial benefit: "Your home is worth more than you think. Here's the equity available."
- **Day 5:** Voice call to discuss both sides (selling current home + buying smaller)
- **Day 7:** Email with case study: "Here's how [similar client] downsized and got MORE for their home"
- **Day 9:** SMS: "Let's look at your options. No pressure — just information."
- **Day 12:** Final outreach: "Ready to start exploring?"

**Customizations:**
- Adjust to your local downsizer profile
- Add financial calculators
- Include lifestyle benefits (less maintenance, more freedom)
- Show "after" communities/neighborhoods

**Expected Results:**
- Response rate: 40-50%
- Consultation booking: 25-35% of responses
- Listing rate: 50-70% of consultations
