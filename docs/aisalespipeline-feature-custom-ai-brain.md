## What is Custom AI Brain?

Custom AI Brain is a training system where your AI learns:
- **Your market:** Neighborhoods, schools, commute times, pricing trends, investment opportunities
- **Your closing style:** How you handle objections, build rapport, explain features, overcome price concerns
- **Your personality:** Communication style, humor, values, what you care about
- **Your process:** Your 7-step home buying process, your 5-step seller interview, your scheduling preferences

Then, when your AI generates SMS, emails, calls, or videos, they sound like YOU — not a generic bot.

## How It Works

### Step 1: Market Training (30 minutes)
You provide:
- Neighborhoods you specialize in (Edison, Millburn, Hoboken, etc.)
- Price ranges you work with
- School districts you know well
- Commute patterns for your area
- Recent market trends you've noticed
- Investment opportunities in your market

Your AI absorbs this. Now when it talks about homes, it references YOUR knowledge.

### Step 2: Style Training (1 hour)
You answer:
- How do you handle price objections?
- What's your elevator pitch?
- How do you build trust with new clients?
- What's your unique value proposition?
- How do you handle competition from big teams?
- What's your closing approach?

Your AI learns your philosophy and approach. Now it negotiates, closes, and persuades like you.

### Step 3: Personality Training (30 minutes)
You provide:
- Your background (how you got into real estate)
- Your values (what matters to you)
- Your personality (formal, casual, funny, serious)
- Your story (what makes you different)
- Your testimonials (what clients say about you)

Your AI becomes you. Not a chatbot. Your representative.

### Step 4: AI Generates Personalized Communications
Now when a lead comes in, your AI:
- **SMS:** "Hi Sarah, saw you looking at homes in Edison — the good school district with the 15-minute commute to NYC. I just listed one that checks all your boxes..."
- **Email:** Personalized to their situation, in your voice, with your insights
- **Voice call:** Uses your phrasing, your approach, your closing techniques
- **Video:** AI Clone Video of you, in your words

Everything feels like it came directly from you — because it did.
