## How It Works

### 1. **Lead Quality Check**
Your AI identifies which leads are worth calling (not everyone needs a voice call). Leads that prefer SMS continue via text.

### 2. **Call Placed**
Your AI calls the lead at an optimal time. The greeting? Personalized. "Hi Sarah, it's about that 3-bed home in Edison you inquired about."

### 3. **Natural Conversation**
The AI engages in genuine dialogue:
- **Lead:** "What's the square footage?"
- **AI:** "It's 2,100 sqft on a quarter-acre lot. What's important to you in a home?"
- **Lead:** "Good schools nearby?"
- **AI:** "Yes, it's in the Edison school district. Top-rated elementary school 2 blocks away."

### 4. **Qualification & Intent**
AI gathers key info:
- Buy timeline? ("Looking in next 30 days" vs. "6 months away")
- Budget range? ("$400-500K" vs. "TBD")
- Must-haves? ("Good schools, quiet area")
- Objections? ("Price seems high" → AI provides market data)

### 5. **Appointment Booking**
If lead is qualified: "Perfect. Let's set up a time for you to see it. I have slots Thursday at 2pm or 4pm. Which works?"

Lead accepts → Appointment confirmed and added to your calendar automatically.

### 6. **Call Summary**
After the call, you get:
- Full transcript
- Lead qualification summary
- Appointment details (if scheduled)
- Objections & AI responses
- Recommended next step
