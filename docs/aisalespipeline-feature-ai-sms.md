## What is AI SMS?

AI SMS is an intelligent text messaging system that automatically sends personalized, natural-sounding SMS messages to every lead the moment they raise their hand. Unlike generic batch-and-blast messaging, AI SMS reads the context of each lead and crafts messages that feel personal, relevant, and timely.

**Key differences from traditional SMS:**
- **Personalized per lead** — Not templates. Your AI learns your style and generates unique messages
- **Contextually aware** — Messages adapt based on how the lead engaged (Zillow search, website form, call inquiry)
- **Instant response** — Sent within seconds of lead capture, when engagement is highest
- **Multi-turn conversations** — The AI can engage in back-and-forth dialogue, not just one-way broadcasts

## How It Works

### 1. **Lead Captured**
A lead submits a form on your website, calls your number, or engages on Zillow. The system instantly captures their info and entry point.

### 2. **AI Analyzes Intent**
Your AI reads signals: What page did they visit? What keywords did they use? Did they call or message? Are they searching for listings or financing?

### 3. **AI Generates Message**
Based on intent analysis, your AI generates a unique, personalized SMS. It sounds like you. It references something specific from their inquiry.

**Example:**
- Lead searched: "3-bed homes in Edison under $500K"
- AI SMS: "Hi Sarah 👋 Saw you looking at 3-beds in Edison. Just got a property listing that matches — only 2 on the market under $500K right now. Worth seeing? [Link to listing]"

### 4. **Lead Responds**
They text back immediately (35-45% do). Your AI engages in natural conversation, asking qualifying questions, scheduling showings, or providing information.

### 5. **Loop Closes or Escalates**
If the lead is qualified, the AI schedules a call with you. If they need nurturing, they enter an automated follow-up sequence.

## Use Cases

### 1. **Expired Listing Follow-Up**
Send personalized SMS to FSBOs and expireds immediately after identifying them.
- "Hi John, your listing on Oak Street expired yesterday. I have 3 buyers actively looking for homes like yours. Let's talk Thursday?"
- **Result:** 25-35% call-back rate

### 2. **Zillow Lead Response**
Instant SMS to anyone who clicks "Contact Agent" on your listings.
- "Hi Margaret! Thanks for your interest in the Millburn home. Quick question: are you looking to buy soon or just browsing?"
- **Result:** 40-50% response rate within 5 minutes

### 3. **Open House Follow-Up**
Auto-send SMS to visitors 1 hour after open house ends.
- "Hey! Thanks for coming to 123 Main St today. Thinking about making an offer, or want to see something else nearby?"
- **Result:** 15-20% turn interested visitors into showings

### 4. **Buyer Lead Nurture**
SMS to new buyer leads to start qualification and move them through pipeline.
- "Hi David, thanks for applying to become a buyer client. 3 questions: 1) Timeline to buy? 2) Budget range? 3) Looking for a home now or in 2-3 months?"
- **Result:** 55-65% completion rate
